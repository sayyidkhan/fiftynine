# 🧠 Project Vision: Investment Brain

---

## 1. Vision

Sembcorp operates as a capital allocator across energy, infrastructure, and sustainability projects.

Today, investment decisions are:

* fragmented across ESG, finance, and operations
* manually stitched together
* slow (weeks → months)
* dependent on meetings, decks, and subjective interpretation

**Investment Brain transforms this into a real-time, AI-native decision system.**

> A system that ingests project inputs and enables structured, explainable, and interactive investment understanding — instantly.

---

## 2. Problem Statement

Internal investment workflows today suffer from:

### **Fragmentation**

* Financial models (IRR, NPV)
* ESG metrics (carbon, compliance)
* Operational constraints (capacity, grid, assets)
  → All live in separate systems

---

### **Human Bottlenecks**

* Analysts compile decks
* Managers review in cycles
* Decisions delayed due to back-and-forth

---

### **Static Decision-Making**

* Evaluations happen at a single point in time
* No continuous re-evaluation as conditions change

---

## 3. Core Idea

> Compress investment understanding from weeks → minutes

Investment Brain acts as:

### 🧠 Decision Engine

Evaluates:

* Financial viability
* Carbon impact
* Operational feasibility
* Risk exposure

---

### 🔁 Simulation Layer

Allows “what-if” scenarios:

* Change energy price
* Change carbon tax
* Change demand assumptions

---

### 📊 Explanation Layer

Outputs:

* Key insights (not forced decisions)
* Transparent reasoning
* Tradeoffs (cost vs carbon vs risk)

---

## 4. Interaction Layer (Swipeable Overview)

> Make investment analysis navigable, not overwhelming

---

### 🃏 Investment Cards (At-a-Glance View)

Each proposal is transformed into a **compact card**:

* 💰 IRR: 11.8%
* 🌱 Carbon Impact: -22%
* ⚠️ Risk: Medium
* ⚡ Strategic Fit: High

**AI Summary:**

> “Strong returns with ESG alignment. Risk from grid dependency.”

---

### 👉 Interaction Model

* 👉 Swipe → Next proposal
* 👈 Swipe → Previous
* ⏸️ Pause → Mark for deeper exploration

🔥 Important:

> Swipe is for **navigation and prioritisation**, not decision-making

---

### 🧠 Outcome

* Scan 20+ proposals in minutes
* Instantly identify:

  * high-potential
  * high-risk
  * worth deeper analysis

---

## 5. Agentic Exploration Layer (Core Differentiator)

> From static analysis → interactive intelligence

---

### 🤖 Agent Mode (Manus-style Interaction)

Clicking into a proposal activates an **AI agent session**.

---

### 🧠 Capabilities

#### 1. Natural Language Exploration

User can ask:

* “Why is risk medium?”
* “What is driving IRR?”

AI responds with:

* structured explanations
* underlying assumptions

---

#### 2. Agentic Commands

User can instruct:

* “Simulate lower energy prices”
* “Compare with similar projects”
* “Optimize for carbon instead of profit”

AI:

* executes analysis
* updates results
* explains impact

---

### 🌌 Parallel Universe Mode (What-if Engine)

> Explore multiple realities of the same investment

---

#### Example:

User:

> “What if energy price drops 15%?”

System:

* recalculates IRR
* updates risk profile
* adjusts insight

---

#### Output:

> “IRR drops to 6%. Risk increases due to revenue sensitivity.”

---

### 🧠 Mental Model

Not:

> “Give me the answer”

But:

> **“Let me explore the decision space”**

---

### 🔁 Agent Loop

1. User modifies assumption
2. AI recomputes
3. AI explains
4. User iterates

---

## 6. User Journey (End-to-End)

### 👤 Persona: Investment Analyst / Strategy Team

---

### Step 1 — Input Project

* Upload proposal (PDF / text)
* Provide key metrics:

  * Capex
  * IRR
  * Emissions
  * Location

---

### Step 2 — AI Structuring

* Extracts key data
* Normalizes into decision-ready format

---

### Step 3 — Swipeable Overview

* View proposals as cards
* Rapidly scan and prioritise

---

### Step 4 — Deep Dive (Agent Mode)

* Click into selected proposal
* Explore reasoning and tradeoffs

---

### Step 5 — Simulation

* Run “what-if” scenarios
* Observe how outcomes shift

---

### Step 6 — Export / Integration

* Generate:

  * investment memo
  * board summary
* Integrate into approval workflows

---
